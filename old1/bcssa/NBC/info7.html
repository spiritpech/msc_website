CNN carried NBC-related news on 8/22

In the morning on 8-22-1996, CNN broadcasted NBC-related news to this effect:

NBC is apologizing to the Chinese and Chinese American groups for the remarks made by Bob Costas at the openning ceremony of the Olympic Games regarding China's human right and copy right issues. In its letter, NBC states: " ...... has no intention to disrepect anyone .... apologizes for ...." Someone is calling NBC to apologize both on air and in writing.
