How we use the balance

    The supervisory committee is accepting applications for proper use of the rest balance from proper organization or person. Please send your application to the committee chair Li Yi at lyi@euler.berkeley.edu or 2299 Piedmont Ave., #395, Berkeley, CA 94720, USA. The flow of the balance will be shown, up to a single penny.

    The principle of granting the fund is that the related activities must be protesting NBC related and must be an appropriate way and positive way to protest or affect. The committee reserve the right not to grant the fund to anyone or oganization which we think is not qualified.

    In your application, please justify why your proposed action can affect NBC positively and effectively. We know Bob Costa will be the commentator of the next Olympics Game.

    The committee has preapproved the qulification of the Chinese American Fundation. But we welcome more applications. The deadline is Nov. 15, 1995. The final result will be announced here on Dec. 1, 1996.
