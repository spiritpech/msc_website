Summary of the telephone conference of the headquater committee In 8/20 afternoon, the committee of the headquater discussed and voted for future plans and strategies of the protest against NBC. The following agreements have been reached by the committee by vote. 1) The apology of NBC in its present form is not acceptable. 2) We will continue to push NBC to make its apology on air. 3) A half page Ad on New York Times will be posted on next Wednesday. 4) A news conference will be held on 8-23, giving NBC a deadline to respond our demand for apology on air. Two version of the Ad on NYT is being developed. Which one will be posted depends on NBC's response of our demand. 5) The headquater admitted two new members.
