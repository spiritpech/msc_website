DIGITAL TRACKING CONTROLLER DESIGN FOR CD PLAYER USING DISTURBUNCE OBSERVER

Koji Fujiyama

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

The optical player has made rapid and remarkable progress in the past 10 years. In addition to CD/CD-ROM, the DVD (Digital Video Disk) player has finally been made available to the market. As these optical players gain in popularity, they have been utilized in various hardware applications such as portable CD player. Presently, one major problem of the CD-ROM/DVD players is vibration caused by the high rotational speed of the spindle motor. In both cases, disturbance suppression control is becoming more and more important. The object of my research is to develop a disturbance observer for a CD player tracking controller and to achieve an improvement in control robustness relative to conventional controllers, which are usually PID or lead-lag.

METHODOLOGY

  • Apply a disturbencs observer to CD player tracking control
  • Develop the disturbance observer algorithm by using fixed-point DSP
  • Evaluate the performance of the disturbance observer through simulations and experiments.

RESULTS TO DATE

  • Installed the vibration testing system
  • Develop a disturbance observer simulation
  • Implemented a disturbance observer with dixed-point DSP
