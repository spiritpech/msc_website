H-infinity and Scaled-H-infinity Optimization of Fixed Structure Controllers
  
Soichi Ibaraki and Masayoshi Tomizuka
 
 
Abstract
  
In this paper, the H-infinity and scaled-H-infinity optimization problems of fixed structure linear controllers are considered. Applications of the problems include tuning of SISO (Single-Input Single-Output) PID (Proportional plus Integral plus Derivative) controller gains. The proposed algorithm starts with transformation of the original problems into a static output feedback controller synthesis problem, which does not impose any constraints on the controller structure except for its order. Unlike the full-order H-infinity controller synthesis case, the H-infinity and scaled-H-infinity optimization problems of static output feedback controllers cannot be reparameterized as a convex optimization problem. The cone complementarity linearization algorithm is used to overcome the nonconvexity problem due to the constraint on the controller order. The proposed algorithm is applied to the design of a SISO PID controller for head positioning of a magnetic hard disk drive.
