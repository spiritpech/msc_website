Network and Printing

Network Information

Our lab no longer provides a local wired or wireless network due to security concerns. Please use the campus Airbears network to connect to the internet.

Printing Information

The MSC Lab has a Brother HL-5450DN Laser Printer (black and white) in 2103 Etcheverry for the convenience of our lab members. The printer is accessible from your personal computer via an internet connection. Please contact Junkai Lu for instructions on how to set up printing capabilities.
